# Privacy Policy  
App: Lista de Tarefas (Task List)

## Introduction  
The **Lista de Tarefas** app was developed with a focus on personal organization and user privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how data is handled within the app and clarifies that no personal information is collected or transmitted to external servers.

## Data Collection and Use  
**Lista de Tarefas does not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal user data.**  
All information entered by the user — such as tasks, reminders, and notes — is used solely for the app’s local functionality.

## Data Storage  
Task data is stored **locally on the user’s device** using **Room (SQLite)**.  
- No information is sent to external servers.  
- No information is synced to the cloud.  
- No data is shared with third parties.

Users may delete their tasks at any time directly within the app.

## Advertising  
During the testing phase, the app uses **AdMob in test mode**, which does not collect real user data.  
After the official release, the app may display ads through **Google AdMob**, which may collect minimal technical data required for ad display, according to Google’s policies.  
The app may also offer an option to **remove ads**.

## Target Audience  
The app is intended for **teenagers and adults**.  
It is not designed for children.

## Security  
Since all data is stored locally on the user’s device, security depends on the protections provided by the Android operating system.  
The app does not access, transmit, or process sensitive information.

## Data Sharing  
**Lista de Tarefas does not share any data** with third parties, except for minimal technical information automatically handled by AdMob (when enabled), according to Google’s policies.

## Data Retention and Deletion  
All data remains on the user’s device until they choose to delete it manually.  
The app does not keep external copies and has no access to the stored data.
